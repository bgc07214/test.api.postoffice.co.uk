Being elected as an independent candidate in Faridpur-3 constituency, Ha-Meem Group Managing Director A. K. The people of Faridpur welcomed Azad. On Saturday night, he was showered with love at the banquet hall of Times Media Bhavan in Tejgaon of the capital. It is jointly organized by ‘Greater Faridpur Friends Association (GFFA)’ and ‘Faridpur AND-U Forum’.<\/p>\n

Under the chairmanship of Rafiqul Islam Jewel, President of Friends Association, President of AND-U Forum, Lt. Col. (retd.) Md. Farooq Azam, General Secretary of Friends Association Engineer Saidur Rahman, Senior Vice President Dr. Abul Hashem, Organizing Secretary and Publisher of Daily Samakal Abul Kalam Azad, Senior Member Rezaul Haider Firoz and others. Sabbir Hossain, Finance Secretary of the Association welcomed the speech. In addition, people from different classes and professions in different areas of Faridpur attended the event. K. He gave a speech congratulating Azad.<\/p>\n

At the event. K. Azad reminisced about his university life. He also tells the story of his rise. He said, I wanted Awami League nomination in Faridpur but I did not get it. I stand in the election due to the people’s demand. Many seniors hugged me and said, ‘I’m going to vote because you’re standing.’ As the days progressed, the support of the people increased. Wherever I went, people would tell me, ‘Don’t think about votes. You just see to it that the vote is fair.’<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
