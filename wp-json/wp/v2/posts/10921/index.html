General Secretary of Awami League and Minister of Road Transport and Bridges Obaidul Quader said that this government is the elected government of the people. China, Russia and India can be our friends. But no foreign power put us in power. The game of election is over, now the game of politics will be played. Corruption, communalism, strike-blockade and fire will be played against terrorism. <\/p>\n

Obaidul Quader said these things while speaking as the chief guest at the peace and democracy rally organized by the Dhaka Metropolitan South Awami League in front of the party’s central office at Bangabandhu Avenue in the capital on Saturday. A large number of leaders and activists participated in this rally organized on the day of black flag march of BNP and its allies. The ruling party staged another showdown in the capital through this post-election peace rally. <\/p>\n

On behalf of Obaidul Quader’s party, announcing the program of peace and democracy rally on January 30 across the country, he said that on that day, the leaders and activists will hold peace and development rallies in cities, districts and upazilas holding red and green flags.
