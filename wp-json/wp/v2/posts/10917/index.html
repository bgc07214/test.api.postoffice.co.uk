As the long-standing crisis in the halls of Maulana Bhasani University of Science and Technology (Mavabiprabi) in Tangail has not been resolved, the general students have locked the gates of three halls and the administrative building. University VC Professor Dr. has been blocked in the administrative building. Farhad Hossain and the teachers in charge of the administration.<\/p>\n

Today, Saturday, the students discussed with the administration throughout the day in view of various demands. But they locked the administrative building at around 8:30 pm as there was no solution in the discussion. As of writing this report, the students’ movement is ongoing. <\/p>\n

Earlier on Saturday afternoon, students first locked the gate of Sheikh Russell Hall. They locked Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman Hall and Public Leader Abdul Mannan Hall in the afternoon and evening respectively.<\/p>\n
