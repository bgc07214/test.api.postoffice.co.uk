BNP leaders and activists started gathering in front of the party’s central office in Nayapaltan from 12 noon to participate in the procession. Leaders and activists in various districts of Dhaka division including various wards and police stations of the capital started coming to Nayapaltan with black flags, banners and festoons.<\/p>\n

As time progressed, Nayapaltan became active with the movement of activists. At that time, along with BNP Chairperson Khaleda Zia, they made the streets noisy with various anti-government slogans.<\/p>\n
